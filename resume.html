Don Cameron - Resume

Summary

System/Security Analyst and Architect with a telecoms and software focus, in technical leadership or pre-sales roles.

Career Description

Don has nearly 30 years experience in the Software Engineering and Telecommunications industries, working in multi-national teams in technical leadership or technical pre-sales roles, in Australia, India (Bangalore), Singapore, Japan, UK, and USA.

Don most recently worked at the National E-Health Transition Authority (NEHTA) as a Solution Architect, and at Boeing, supporting AIR 5077 5A (Wedgetail AEWC Interoperability Compliance Upgrade) project ramp-up.

Don has worked in the Defence industry on large communications-related Defence Projects, in a System Design Manager, or System Architect role. Examples include the RAAF Air Defence Ground Environment project (radar sensor fusion), the HF Modernisation Project (communications network management), and the Australian Army Tactical Command Support System project. These projects involved significant requirements analysis and design, and also covered Security Engineering and Safety Engineering Management tasks.

A typical role was being the System Architect within an Air Defence Ground Environment (ADGE) studies team, tasked with analysing and incrementally enhancing the RAAF ADGE.

Don's experience also includes developing and delivering technology transfer training, developing system architectures for automated service activation in Telco Operational Support Systems (OSS), and hands-on programming for the rapid production of prototypes and proof-of-concept systems.

In a Solution Architect role with Astracon Inc., Don's experience, and his ability to transfer technology on technical topics with authority, contributed to the establishment of commercial relationships with HP, Cisco, and NTT (Japan). A typical engagement was the on-site interfacing of Java-based service automation systems to Cisco Optical Network Elements; Don developed a prototype on time, and delivered design and code walkthroughs to the customer's satisfaction.

Don has also worked for Telstra, as a Business Analyst in the Strategy and Coordination Group, Computer Support Services, Network Engineering.
